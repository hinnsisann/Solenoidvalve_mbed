## Issue
The circuit itself appeared fine, but the solenoid valve failed to operate for some reason.

## Solution
I had been attempting to supply the signal to the transistor (used for driving the valve) from the PA_5 pin.

On Brain Board ver.0, the Nucleo F446E's PA_5 pin was connected to OUT_0 rather than LD_0.

Switching the connection to LD_0 resolved the issue, and the system operated correctly.

## Outcome
I implemented the circuit from the breadboard version onto an actual universal board.
